About Me

Growing up in Melbourne Florida for most of my life, I have always held a fascination with computers ever since I was a kid. This fascination went from trying out different software and learning about computer peripherals to making programs in Python and other programming languages. In addition to coding and academics, I was also an active Boy Scout - participating in Troops 314 and 716 with various campouts and volunteer services. In Troop 314 I would earn my Eagle Scout and in Troop 716 I would assist in the Phap Vu Cultural Centers' festivities.

Currently, I am a computer science student at the Florida Institute of Technology, entering in August 2021 and expecting to graduate in May 2025. While at Florida Tech I have taken numerous classes that have expanded my skills at software development, and I have also participated in the Florida Tech Rescue Squad, an organization that is dedicated to providing medical aid to students in need.
